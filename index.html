About Me

There's this spark in her eyes when technology is mentioned and the fire that burns in her soul when undertaking tasks that concerns technology. She has a deep passion for Mental health and loves to learn about the mind, why we think and behave the way we do. But most importantly, she wants to research into schizophrenia and possibly find a way people can actually understand people with it. Essah has won an award during a competition organized by vodafone and is a Course Representative in her university. She also emerged Top 10 in the Huawei Seeds of the Future Initiative exams. Earned the Title of Miss Eloquent in a school pageant. Akua has always believed in discipline and commitment, which is why she ranked Corporal in her junior high school cadet and Sergeant in Senior High School cadet corps. She is an aspiring Medical research scientist (Cognitive Science-Schizophrenia) who is bent on making the world very simple to live in. Essah Akua served as an executive ambassador for Developers In Vogue in her school. Exploring her horizons, connections and network, Akua hopes to reach as many people as she can and make an impact in every life she comes into contact with. She mentors upcoming individuals who are also passionate about technology and want to know more about life.
